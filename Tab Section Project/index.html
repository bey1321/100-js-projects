Step 1: Prepare and Mix the Batter Carefully

  • Make sure your butter, eggs, and milk are at room temperature for a smooth consistency.
  • Cream the butter and sugar together until the mixture becomes light and fluffy. Gradually add eggs one by one, ensuring each is well incorporated.
  • Sift the flour, baking powder, and salt, then alternate adding the dry mixture and milk to the batter. Stir gently to avoid overmixing, as this will help keep the cake soft.

Step 2: Bake the Cake Properly

  • Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F) and prepare your pan by greasing it and lining it with parchment paper.
  • Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pan and lightly tap it to release any trapped air bubbles.
  • Place the pan in the oven and bake for the recommended time or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Be careful not to overbake, as this can dry out the cake.

Step 3: Cool, Decorate, and Serve

  • Once the cake is baked, let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes before carefully transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
  • After the cake has fully cooled, you can decorate it with your choice of frosting, whipped cream, or a dusting of powdered sugar. Slice and serve your deliciously soft cake to enjoy!
